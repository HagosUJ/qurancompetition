About Majlisu Ahlil Qur'an

A faith-based initiative dedicated to fostering the love, memorization, and understanding of the Holy Qur'an.

Empowering generations through the Qur'an.

Majlisu Ahlil Qur'an Initiative is committed to preserving the Qur'anic tradition globally.

We are a non-profit organization rooted in Islamic principles, striving to promote Qur'anic literacy, memorization, and understanding across communities. Our mission is to empower individuals by connecting them with the light and guidance of the Holy Qur'an through innovative programs and global engagement.

By organizing competitions, camps, and digital platforms, we aim to mentor Qur'anic scholars, recognize excellence, and build a global network of Qur'an lovers. Our work bridges traditional Islamic knowledge with modern technology to make Qur'anic learning accessible to all.

With sincerity, excellence, and service at our core, we serve the Ummah with humility, fostering unity and spreading beneficial knowledge.

From Vision to Global Impact

Majlisu Ahlil Qur'an began with a simple vision: to make the Qur'an a living part of every heart.

Founded by a group of dedicated scholars and community leaders, our initiative grew from local Qur'anic circles to an international platform. Today, we host global competitions, develop digital learning tools, and empower communities worldwide, all while staying true to our core values of sincerity, excellence, and service.

Our Key Initiatives

We offer a range of programs to promote Qur'anic engagement and excellence.

Our initiatives are designed to inspire and empower learners of all ages. From international competitions to digital platforms, we provide opportunities for individuals to connect with the Qur'an in meaningful ways. Our key programs include:

  • Qur'an Competition.

    Our annual International Qur'an Competition showcases excellence in Tajweed, Hifz, and Tafsir, bringing together young memorizers and reciters from around the world.

  • Qur'anic Camps.

    We organize camps and retreats to spiritually uplift participants through intensive Qur'anic learning and reflection, fostering a deeper connection with the Qur'an.

  • Women & Children Programs.

    Special initiatives focus on empowering female memorizers and young learners, providing tailored support to nurture their Qur'anic journey.

  • Digital Platforms.

    We develop apps and websites to make Qur'anic resources accessible, enabling learners worldwide to engage with the Qur'an anytime, anywhere.

  • Teacher Development.

    Our workshops train Qur'an instructors to enhance teaching quality, ensuring effective and impactful learning experiences for students.

Our Core Values

At Majlisu Ahlil Qur'an, we are driven by:

Ikhlas (Sincerity).

Acting solely for the sake of Allah in all our endeavors.

Ihsan (Excellence).

Upholding high standards in every activity we undertake.

Wahda (Unity).

Promoting Islamic brotherhood and togetherness.

Khidmah (Service).

Serving the Ummah with humility and care.
